Lead Qualification

Score Threshold

Each AI reply adds or subtracts up to 20 points based on lead engagement. When the cumulative score hits this number, the booking link is sent exactly once. Recommended: 60–75.

Booking Link

Sent automatically once when a lead's score reaches the threshold. Include UTM params if you're tracking conversions.

Lead Scoring Rules

Define what signals increase or decrease the lead score. For example: "Sharing a diagnosis should increase urgency. Expressing cost concerns without engagement should reduce readiness." The AI uses this guidance when classifying each conversation turn.
